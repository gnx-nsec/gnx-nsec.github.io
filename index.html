ABOUT US

Who are we?

GNX is the official GNU/Linux User Group of Netaji Subhash Engineering College, a community of linux loving & open-source enthusiastic people.

By the way,why 'GNX's Not niXal'?, well it has a pretty interesting story behind it! ;)

What made us form this group?

GNX was founded in 2018 with the aim of providing support to the individuals for contributing to opensouce and gaining knowledge about Linux.Our main motto is to promote the adoption of Linux, encourage others to actively contribute to the opensouce projects and build a helpful community of like-minded people.

What drives us?

Contributing to opensouce has a lot of benefits, it can really boost the learning and programming experience of a developer very quickly.An opensouce developer always gets an edge over the other developers.So promoting the opensouce culture is what that propels this group.

GNX also offers guidance and support to the developers for the various paid summer of code programmes.
